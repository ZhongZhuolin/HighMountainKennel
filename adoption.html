We breed AKC-Registered and pedigreed Vizslas and German Short-Haired Pointers. Our dams and sires come from outstanding bloodlines—perfect for bird-hunting, obedience work, and, above all, as affectionate family companions. Contact us for more details!

Vizslas

The Vizsla is a gentle, loyal sporting breed known for its striking rust-colored coat. Bred in Hungary as a versatile pointing dog, Vizslas excel in the field yet thrive in the home—eager to please, highly trainable, and wonderfully affectionate with children.

German Short-Haired Pointers

Renowned for versatility, the German Short-Haired Pointer (GSP) combines athleticism with intelligence. Their keen noses and boundless energy make them exceptional upland and waterfowl hunters, while their friendly, even-tempered nature makes them beloved family dogs.
