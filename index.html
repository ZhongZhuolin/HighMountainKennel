About Us

We are a family owned dog kennel and boarding business located in Warren County, New Jersey. We pride ourselves in our small yet homey facility, with constant care for your animal so you can enjoy your time, whether you're on vacation or need a place to take care of your pet. We do overnight stays to weeklong stays at our kennel, please check out our contact info below and inquire us for further details.

Book Your Stay View All Services

Short & Long-Term Boarding

We offer both overnight and weeklong stays, a doggie daycare, and even supervised swim time in our pool, in a safe, homey environment! Your dog will enjoy a comfortable suite, daily exercise, and lots of love and attention.

Play & Care

Dogs enjoy supervised playtime, socialization, and enrichment activities every day. We separate dogs by size and temperament for safety and fun.

Preparing for Boarding

We recommend a short visit or daycare session before your dog's first overnight stay to help them adjust. Please bring your dog's food, any medication, and let us know about their routines.

What to Bring

Bring your dog's food, any medication, and bedding or comfort items. We provide bowls, toys, and lots of love!

After Boarding

After a fun stay, your dog may be tired and thirsty. Let them rest and rehydrate at home.
